Where a gnome can find a home

Here at GnomaPalooza Old Gnome Sanctuary, we care immensely for each and every garden-dwelling friend that comes through our doors. Our family is committed to providing only the best care: With diets, training, and living quarters formulated by gnome experts across the globe. Though we specialize in taking care of gnomes in need, gnomes of any age and background are welcome in GnomaPalooza Old Gnome Sanctuary.

Who are we?

Founded in 2013, our staff is comprised of gnome-lovers and enthusiasts. GnomaPalooza Old Gnome Sanctuary was created with one idea in mind: to give care to those gnomes who need it. Gnomes can generally take care of themselves in the wild, but too often are domesticated gnomes left uncared for and unable to fend for themselves. Old or otherwise "undesirable" gnomes are also abandoned quite often. This is where we come in. Meet some of our staff below!

Kaitlyn has been with GnomaPalooza since its beginning! As one of our founders, there's no surprise that she has an endless supply of love and care for every gnome and is excellent at communicating with them. Definitely one of our gnome's favorite caretakers, for good reason.

The other founder of GnomaPalooza, Joey, has never come across a gnome in need that he wasn't able to help. In addition, Joey handles most of the marketing and has done so since our founding. Whether it's business needs or a gnome in distress, he's sure to get the job done!

Curtis joined our organization back in 2015. A professional wrangler, any unruly or aggressive gnomes are soon to be docile in Curt's capable hands. This valued member of our family uses advanced psychology to determine each gnome's needs and stressors.

GnomaPalooza Old Gnome Sanctuary wouldn't be as safe as it is without our bodyguard, Aaron! Aaron is a fairly new member to our group, though in the months working here, he has shown nothing but dedication and commitment to making sure staff and gnome alike feel safe.
